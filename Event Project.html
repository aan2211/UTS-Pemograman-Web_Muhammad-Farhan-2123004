OLYMPUS 2.0

mascot character design

Tarsiers are nocturnal animals, and are often considered detrimental to garden owners, even though these animals are very helpful in cleaning up pests in their gardens at night. Therefore, this animal fits perfectly with the Olympus 2.0 theme, namely invisible but impactful.

Sketch

The mascot character design itself uses an anthropomorphic style, with the characteristics of the Tarsier being maintained, namely wide eyes, unique earlobes, a long tail, and long hands and feet.

Final Design

In accordance with the theme and habits of these animals, the costumes are made using robes with head coverings like the Batman and Moon Knight characters, this is suitable because they are active at night. Then wear a suit that is slim and tight so that the silhouette of your body proportions is visible, don't use various attributes and add bright line accents. In the middle of the chest, there is the Olympus logo which is integrated with the robe, which is intended as an identity and symbol for the mascot.

Maskot Poses

The costume colors used are blue, purple and dark grey. This color has a cool tone and this color is suitable and is often used at night, considering that this mascot is a hero who moves at night. And the yellow color gives a neon and vibrant impression but is not excessive. Its tail has a texture gradient to yellow as a form of strength when in action
